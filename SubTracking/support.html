FAQ

Why does the monthly total look wrong?

Amounts are entered manually. Weekly and yearly subscriptions are converted into a monthly equivalent. SubTracking v1.0 does not perform foreign exchange conversion, so mixed-currency totals should be treated as estimates only.

Why am I not receiving renewal reminders?

Open iOS Settings → Notifications → SubTracking and allow notifications. Also make sure renewal reminders are enabled for the subscription inside SubTracking.

How many subscriptions can I add?

SubTracking v1.0 has no subscription count limit.

Can SubTracking connect to my bank?

No. SubTracking does not connect to banks, cards, payment accounts, emails, receipts, or billing statements. All subscription records are entered manually.

Important Notice

SubTracking is a personal subscription tracking tool. It is not a bank, financial institution, payment service, accounting service, or investment adviser. Amounts and renewal dates are manually entered by users and are provided for personal reference only.

中文常见问题

月合计不对？

金额为手动填写；年/周订阅会折算为月等价。SubTracking v1.0 不做汇率换算，多币种混合合计仅适合作为估算参考。

收不到续费提醒？

请在 iOS 设置 → 通知 → SubTracking 中允许通知，并在添加或编辑订阅时开启“续费提醒”。

能加多少条订阅？

SubTracking v1.0 无数量限制。

能连银行吗？

不能。SubTracking 不连接银行，也不读取邮件账单。所有订阅信息都需要用户手动录入。
