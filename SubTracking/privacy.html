SubTrack Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

Last Updated: July 4, 2026

Operator: wangyabo ("we", "us")

Contact: contackworking@163.com

This Privacy Policy explains how wangyabo ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with SubTrack ("SubTrack" or the "App"). SubTrack is designed as a local-first personal subscription tracker. The current release does not require an account and does not send your subscription records to our servers.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the SubTrack iOS app and related support communications. It does not apply to third-party services, websites, subscription providers, or Apple services that are governed by their own privacy policies.

2. Information Processed by the App

SubTrack processes the following information locally on your device when you choose to enter or enable it:

  • Subscription records you manually create: subscription name, icon or emoji, amount, currency, billing cycle, next renewal date, category, reminder preference, and creation date.
  • Calculated app information: estimated monthly totals, renewal calendar entries, upcoming renewal lists, and chart data calculated from the subscription records you enter.
  • Notification preferences: whether renewal reminders are enabled for individual subscriptions.

These records are stored on your device using Apple's local app storage technologies. We do not operate an account system for the current release.

3. Support Communications

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address, the content of your message, and any information you choose to include. We use this information only to respond to your request, troubleshoot issues, and improve support.

Please avoid sending sensitive financial account details, passwords, payment card numbers, or other confidential information in support emails.

4. How Information Is Used

SubTrack uses information processed in the App to:

  • Save and display your subscription list.
  • Calculate estimated monthly subscription spending based on your entries.
  • Show upcoming renewals, calendar views, and spending charts.
  • Schedule optional local reminders before renewal dates.
  • Let you edit, delete, and manage your subscription records.

Support emails are used to respond to support requests and maintain basic support records.

5. Information We Do Not Collect Through the App

In the current release, SubTrack does not:

  • Require account registration or login.
  • Connect to banks, cards, payment accounts, financial institutions, or subscription providers.
  • Read emails, receipts, bank statements, transaction history, or billing history.
  • Upload your subscription records to our servers.
  • Collect location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, calendar, or HealthKit data.
  • Use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking SDKs.
  • Track you across apps or websites.
  • Collect a remote push notification token for server-side messaging.

SubTrack's privacy manifest declares no tracking and no data collection by us through the App for the current release.

6. Notifications

SubTrack may request permission to send notifications. Notifications are used only for local renewal reminders before subscription renewal dates.

If you deny or later disable notification permission, you can still use the App to manually track subscriptions and view renewal dates. Notification delivery depends on your device settings and iOS behavior.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your locally stored subscription records with advertisers, analytics providers, data brokers, or financial institutions.

We may disclose support communications if required by law, legal process, or a valid governmental request, or when reasonably necessary to protect our rights, users, or the security of the App.

8. Third-Party Services

The current release relies on Apple platform services, including:

  • Apple iOS local storage technologies for saving app data on your device.
  • Apple UserNotifications for optional local renewal reminders.
  • Apple App Store for app distribution and updates.

These services are provided by Apple and may be subject to Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Subscription records remain on your device until you edit or delete them in the App, or delete the App from your device. Deleting the App may delete local app data, subject to iOS behavior and any device backup settings you control.

Support emails may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to you, maintain support history, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

10. Your Choices

You can:

  • Add, edit, or delete subscription records in the App.
  • Turn renewal reminders on or off for individual subscriptions.
  • Disable notification permission in iOS Settings.
  • Delete the App from your device.
  • Contact us about privacy questions at contackworking@163.com.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional privacy rights under applicable law. You may contact us to request assistance with those rights.

11. Data Security

We design SubTrack to keep subscription records on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure. You should protect your device with appropriate security settings, such as a passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

12. Children's Privacy

SubTrack is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information through support communications, please contact us.

13. Financial Disclaimer

SubTrack is a personal tracking tool. It is not a bank, financial institution, payment service, accounting service, or investment adviser.

All amounts and renewal dates are manually entered by you and are provided for personal reference only. You are responsible for checking actual charges, renewal dates, cancellation requirements, and billing terms with the relevant service providers.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, provide notice through the App or our website.
