Privacy Policy

CalmSprint · Effective date: July 21, 2026 · Last updated: July 21, 2026 · Version 1.0.0

Summary: CalmSprint is a local-first focus timer for iPhone. We do not create accounts, do not operate a CalmSprint server that receives your tasks or timer data, and do not use advertising or analytics SDKs. Your content stays on your device unless you choose to share it.

2. Scope

This Policy applies to CalmSprint for iPhone (bundle ID com.selflab.calmsprint) and its Widget / Live Activity extension (com.selflab.calmsprint.widget). It also applies to this Privacy Policy webpage.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

In version 1.0, we do not:

  • Require sign-in or create user accounts
  • Transmit your tasks, focus sessions, settings, or statistics to a CalmSprint backend server
  • Use third-party advertising, attribution, analytics, or crash-reporting SDKs
  • Sell personal information or share it with data brokers
  • Track you across apps or websites for advertising (the App does not request App Tracking Transparency)
  • Collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, health, or motion data

For App Store Connect “App Privacy”, version 1.0 is intended to be declared as Data Not Collected (no tracking), unless a future binary adds collection practices.

4. Information Stored on Your Device

To provide core features, the App stores information locally on your device (and, when available, in the App Group group.com.selflab.calmsprint so widgets and Live Activities on the same device can read a local snapshot):

Category Examples Purpose
Tasks Title, optional note, tag, estimated sessions, completion state Task list and binding a task to a focus session
Focus sessions Timestamps, phase type, duration, linked task (if any) Statistics, streaks, charts, rankings
Timer state Current phase, remaining/anchor time, run state Resume after leaving the App; widgets / Live Activities
Settings Durations, daily goal, notification preferences, theme, language Personalize App behavior and appearance

This on-device information is processed by the App for functionality. It is not uploaded by us to a CalmSprint server. If you enable Apple device backups (for example, iCloud Backup), Apple may include App data in those backups under Apple’s terms and your device settings. Uninstalling the App typically deletes the App’s local data from the device.

5. Permissions and System Features

Feature What happens
Local Notifications If you grant permission, iOS schedules local alerts for phase completion and optional monthly reminders. Notification content is generated on device. We do not send remote push notifications from a server.
Live Activities Shows an on-device countdown on the Lock Screen / Dynamic Island where supported. The App does not use push-to-update Live Activities.
Background refresh May help settle an expired timer phase when the App is backgrounded, subject to iOS scheduling.
Share Sheet If you share a statistics card, iOS presents the system share sheet. Destinations you choose (Messages, Photos, etc.) are controlled by you and those apps’ privacy policies.
Deep links The App registers the calmsprint:// URL scheme for opening tabs from widgets (for example, timer, tasks, stats, settings).

8. International Users

Processing described in this Policy for App content occurs on your device. If you email us, your message is processed where our email service and we handle support correspondence.

9. Your Choices

10. Data Retention

We do not retain your CalmSprint App content on a Publisher server. Retention on your device lasts until you delete the data or uninstall the App, subject to any on-device pruning of older sessions and any backups you configure with Apple or your device. Support emails are retained only as needed to handle your request and for ordinary business records.

11. Security

On-device data is protected by iOS sandboxing and your device passcode / biometrics as configured by you. No method of storage is perfectly secure; protect your device and backups accordingly.
