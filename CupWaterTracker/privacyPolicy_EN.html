Cup Water Track (also known as 喝水侠 in Chinese locales, "the App") values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information We Process

Based on the current release, we process:

  1. Local app data (on-device)
    • Hydration logs (time, amount, drink type)
    • Preferences such as daily goal, reminder time, onboarding status
  2. HealthKit data (only with your permission)
    • Read/write hydration data to display and sync progress
  3. Notification permission status
    • Used for scheduled local hydration reminders (not remote push)
  4. In-app purchase status (StoreKit)
    • Monthly, yearly, or lifetime Pro entitlement state; purchase and restore results (payments handled by Apple)
  5. Widget shared data (on-device, only after you add a Home Screen widget)
    • Today's hydration summary (intake, goal, last updated) shared between the main app and the widget extension via Apple App Group
    • This data stays on your device; we do not upload it to our servers

We do not require account registration and do not intentionally collect direct identifiers such as government IDs. We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

2. How We Use Information

We use information only to:

  • Provide and maintain hydration tracking, statistics, and reminders;
  • Sync hydration data with Apple Health when authorized;
  • Display today's hydration progress in the Home Screen widget for Pro users;
  • Handle Pro entitlement checks, purchases, and restores;
  • Troubleshoot and improve app reliability.

3. Permissions

  • HealthKit: Used only after your consent; core local logging still works if denied.
  • Notifications: Used for local hydration reminders; manual logging still works if denied.
  • StoreKit: Apple handles payment processing; we do not store card details.
  • WidgetKit / App Group: Used only after you add a widget, to share a hydration snapshot locally between the app and widget extension; no network transfer involved.

4. Storage and Security

  • The current version stores data primarily on your device (SwiftData / UserDefaults / App Group container).
  • We do not operate our own backend servers for your hydration logs.
  • We apply reasonable safeguards, but no method is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

Current Apple platform integrations:

  • Apple HealthKit (with your consent)
  • Apple StoreKit / App Store
  • Apple UserNotifications (local notifications)
  • Apple WidgetKit (Home Screen widget, Pro feature)

We do not sell personal information.

6. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13.

7. Your Choices

You can:

  • Disable HealthKit or Notifications in iOS Settings;
  • Remove the Home Screen widget to stop widget-related App Group display;
  • Uninstall the App to remove local logs and preferences (subject to iOS behavior);
  • Manage data already written to Apple Health in the iOS Health app.

The current version does not provide per-entry delete in the App; uninstalling is the primary way to remove local data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as features or legal requirements evolve. Material changes will be announced in the App or on our website.

9. Medical Disclaimer

The App is a wellness habit tool and does not provide medical advice.
